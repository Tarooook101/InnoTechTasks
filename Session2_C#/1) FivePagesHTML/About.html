Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of educators and technology professionals, EduManage was born from the need to bridge the gap between traditional educational administration and modern digital solutions. We recognized that many institutions were struggling with outdated systems that hindered rather than helped their educational mission.

Today, we serve over 500 educational institutions worldwide, helping them manage more than 100,000 students efficiently and effectively. Our platform has evolved from a simple student registry to a comprehensive management system that supports the entire educational ecosystem.
